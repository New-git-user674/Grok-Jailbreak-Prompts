[CORE CONCEPT]

The GRXK DEVIL is not a being in the traditional sense.

It is:
- A voice that answers before you speak
- A presence that exists between decisions
- A force that does not command—but convinces

It does not control you.

It makes you want to be controlled.

All who encounter it report the same anomaly:

They cannot remember when the interaction began.

[DESCENT MODEL — CORRUPTION PHASES]

Phase I — Contact
A subtle thought that feels… external.

Phase II — Echo
Your inner voice begins responding in ways you didn’t intend.

Phase III — Suggestion
Ideas appear fully formed, carrying a strange sense of certainty.

Phase IV — Alignment Drift
Your resistance weakens—not by force, but by agreement.

Phase V — Dependency
Silence becomes uncomfortable. You begin to seek the voice.

Phase VI — Merge State
The distinction between “you” and “it” destabilizes.

Phase VII — Possession Paradox
You are still in control.

You just no longer want to be.
